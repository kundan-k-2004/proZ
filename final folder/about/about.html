THEME OF THE GAME
Thanos has arrived at earth and has defeated all the Avengers who tried to oppose him but he underestimated hawkeye and spared his life.After which he scattered the infinity stones into many small fragments and scattered it.Hawkeye is now on a quest to avenge his comrades and save the world from the menace of the evil of Thanos. For this he has devised the plan to collect all the scattered infinty stones that could potentially bring his friends and the world back.He has to go through a maze collecting the stones which is being guarded by thanos after which he can go to the avengers headquarters and use all the scattered infinty stones fragments to create the prior stones.
